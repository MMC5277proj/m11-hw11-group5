Ready Player One Characters

Parzival

Parzival is the avatar of Wade Watts, who is a talented gamer with a vast knowledge of 80s pop culture. He sets out on a mission to discover an Easter egg hidden by the platform’s creator and whoever finds the egg inherits control of the OASIS and a massive fortune. Parzival stands out for his ingenuity, and his willingness to take on powerful rivals...

Art3mis

Art3mis is the avatar of Samantha Cook in Ready Player One. She’s a smart and famous egg hunter in the OASIS, known for her skills and independence. Like Parzival, she’s focused on finding Halliday’s Easter egg, but she’s also concerned about making sure the OASIS doesn’t end up controlled by a big corporation.

Sorrento

Sorrento is the main villain in Ready Player One. He’s the boss of a big company called IOI that wants to take over the OASIS, the huge virtual reality world in the story. Sorrento is cutthroat, and will do anything to find the Easter egg hidden in the OASIS, which would give him total control of it.

Aech

Aech from Ready Player One is the avatar of Helen, Wade’s best friend in the virtual world called the OASIS. Online, Aech looks like a big, strong male character, but in real life, Helen is actually a woman. She hides her real identity to stay safe and avoid prejudice. Aech is smart, skilled, and always helps Wade on their adventure to find a hidden prize in the game.

I-R0K

I-R0k is a hired gun and treasure hunter working for the antagonist, Nolan Sorrento. He’s cocky and loves to flaunt his collection of rare items and gear in the OASIS. Despite trying to act tough and menacing, he often comes off as clumsy and arrogant.

Curator

The Curator is an android in the OASIS who takes care of the Halliday Journals, a collection of memories from James Halliday’s life. He first seems annoyed with Parzival's constant visits, but plays a vital rule in Parzival's success.

Anorak

Anorak is the avatar of James Halliday, who made the OASIS. He looks like a wizard in robes. Anorak is the one who created the big contest where players try to find a hidden Easter egg in the game. The winner gets control of the OASIS and Halliday’s fortune.

Sho

Sho is a skilled player and part of the High Five, the main group of friends in the story. He’s good at gaming and often shows off his skills to hopefully distract from the fact that he is much younger than the rest of the group.

Daito

Daito is a part of the High Five. He’s really good at gaming and knows martial arts. Daito is loyal to his friends and helps them in their quest to find Halliday’s Easter egg.

F'Nale Zandor

F’nale Zandor is a secondary antagonist that works for Sorrento. She is responsible for falsely creating reasons to arrest people and require them to work off their debts in the OASIS. She plays a vital role in trying to derail Parzival's quest.
