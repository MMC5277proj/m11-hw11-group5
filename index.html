Ready Player One is a science fiction story originally authored by Ernest Cline. Set in a dystopian future, the story follows Wade Watts, a teen who spends most of his time gaming in “OASIS,” a virtual reality universe where people can go to escape the bleak real world. When the creator of OASIS dies, he leaved behind clues to an Easter egg which will grant the player who finds it immense wealth and control over OASIS. Along with his friends, he heads out on his quest to solve the puzzles and find the egg.
