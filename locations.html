Real World Locations

In 2045, Columbus, Ohio has transformed into a dystopian urban landscape, ravaged by the effects of climate change and economic collapse. At the heart of this bleak future are "The Stacks," towering vertical trailer parks that have become the primary housing solution for the city's impoverished residents. These precarious structures consist of mobile homes and shipping containers stacked atop one another, held together by a framework of metal scaffolding. The Stacks stretch skyward, creating a chaotic and claustrophobic environment where people live in cramped, makeshift dwellings with little privacy or safety. Despite the harsh living conditions, The Stacks have fostered a sense of community among their inhabitants, who rely on each other for support in this challenging world. The stark contrast between the grim reality of Columbus and the limitless possibilities of the OASIS virtual world serves as a powerful backdrop for the story of Ready Player One, highlighting the escapism and hope that virtual reality offers to those trapped in a decaying physical world.

OASIS Virtual Locations

Ludus

Ludus is a vibrant, fantastical planet where Wade Watts, known by his avatar Parzival, attends virtual school. The planet's most notable feature is its expansive racetrack, which becomes the setting for the film's thrilling first challenge. This high-stakes virtual race, filled with outrageous obstacles and pop culture references, sets the stage for Wade's journey to find Halliday's Easter egg. Ludus showcases the OASIS's ability to blend education with entertainment, creating a visually stunning and action-packed environment that captures the essence of the virtual world's limitless possibilities.

Zero-gravity Dance Club

A zero-gravity dance club where avatars can float and dance freely in a weightless environment. This club serves as a significant setting where Parzival, encounters Art3mis for the first time in person. The club's futuristic design, pulsing music, and floating dancers create a visually stunning and immersive scene. This meeting at The Distracted Globe marks an important moment in Wade and Art3mis's relationship, blending the excitement of virtual reality with the thrill of a budding romance.

The Overlook Hotel

A virtual recreation of the iconic setting from Stanley Kubrick's "The Shining". It's used as the location for the second challenge in Halliday's contest. The OASIS version faithfully replicates the eerie atmosphere and layout of the original film's hotel, including its maze-like corridors and infamous Room 237. Contestants must navigate this virtual haunted hotel, facing recreated scenes and horrors from "The Shining" while searching for the next key in their quest.

Castle Anorak

Castle Anorak, Halliday's fortress on Planet Doom, serves as the dramatic setting for the final challenge. This imposing virtual structure, inspired by Tolkien's Mount Doom, houses the ultimate test for the High Five. Inside the castle, an Atari 2600 console awaits, where Parzival must play Adventure to find the hidden Easter egg - Warren Robinett's name. This final task embodies Halliday's lesson about appreciating the journey and recognizing the creators behind the games, rather than simply focusing on winning
