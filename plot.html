PLOT

In 2045, people escape their harsh realities by immersing themselves in the OASIS (Ontologically Anthropocentric Sensory Immersive Simulation), a virtual entertainment universe created by James Halliday and Ogden Morrow of Gregarious Games.

After Halliday's death, his avatar Anorak announces a contest in a pre-recorded message, promising ownership of the OASIS to the first person who finds the golden Easter egg. This egg is hidden behind a gate that requires three keys, obtainable by completing three challenges. The contest attracts many "Gunters" (egg hunters) and catches the attention of Nolan Sorrento, the CEO of Innovative Online Industries (IOI), who seeks to control the OASIS for profit, using intrusive ads and a team of indentured players known as "Sixers" to hunt for the egg.

Wade Watts, a teenage orphan who goes by the avatar Parzival, is a dedicated Gunter. He participates in the first challenge, a difficult race, alongside his best friend Aech and Art3mis, a female avatar he likes. Parzival often visits Halliday's Journals, a simulated archive of Halliday's life curated by the Curator. Wade wins the Copper Key by cleverly driving backward during the race, followed by Aech, Art3mis, and friends Daito and Sho, who later form the High-5 on the OASIS scoreboard.

Sorrento hires the mercenary i-R0k to uncover Wade's real identity, hoping to bribe him to win the contest for IOI. Wade and Art3mis discover in the Journals that Halliday once dated Kira, Morrow's wife. While searching for clues at the Distracted Globe nightclub, Wade confesses his feelings and true name to Art3mis. They narrowly escape an IOI raid, but Art3mis leaves Wade, citing her father's debt to IOI. i-R0k overhears their conversation and informs Sorrento, who then approaches Wade with a proposal. When Wade refuses, Sorrento retaliates by bombing Wade's home, killing his aunt Alice and her boyfriend Rick.

Samantha Cook, the player behind Art3mis, takes Wade in. Together, they realize that the second challenge is tied to Halliday's regret about not pursuing Kira. Along with Aech, Daito, and Sho, they search for a recreation of the Overlook Hotel. Art3mis asks Kira to dance and wins the Jade Key. However, Sorrento's associate F'Nale Zandor storms their hideout and captures Samantha to settle her father's debt. Wade escapes with help from the other High-5 members in Helen's truck. Samantha manages to escape confinement after Aech and Parzival hack into Sorrento's OASIS setup and record his confession about the bombing.

The third challenge takes place in Castle Anorak on Planet Doom, where players must guess Halliday's favorite Atari 2600 game to earn the Crystal Key. i-R0k creates a force field around the castle using the Orb of Osuvox, but Art3mis disables it. The High-5 rallies an army of OASIS players against IOI. Parzival accidentally kills Samantha's avatar, allowing her to escape with the help of the High-5. A confrontation between Parzival and Sorrento leads to Sorrento detonating the Cataclyst bomb, wiping out all avatars on Planet Doom, including his own.

Parzival respawns thanks to an extra life coin given to him by the Curator. He plays *Adventure* and wins the Crystal Key by finding Warren Robinett's Easter egg. Using all three keys, he enters a treasure room where Anorak presents him with a contract to sign. Parzival recognizes it as the one Morrow had to sign when Halliday forced him out of Gregarious and declines. Anorak then transforms into Halliday, who shares his regrets and awards Parzival the Easter egg.

Sorrento and F'Nale are arrested for the bombing, and Morrow reveals himself as the Curator. Wade decides to run the OASIS with the High-5, inviting Morrow to join them as a consultant. As IOI's Loyalty Centers are shut down, the High-5 controversially opts to close the OASIS every Tuesday and Thursday, encouraging people to spend more time in the real world. Wade and Samantha begin a romantic relationship during this time.

The Ready Player One movie is based on the book of the same name by Ernest Cline, with Zak Penn collaborating with the original author on the script.
